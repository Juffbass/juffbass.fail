Emptiness Reigns - 2023

The lastest JuffBass album is one of complete unique instrumentation, full sounds and emotional and heavy tunes as an authentic post-rock album.

Monolith - 2022

This album is an one-of-a-kind for its huge focus on guitar progressions and more upbeat drums.

Farewell Flight - 2021

The fourth JuffBass album marks a changing point in his sound, serving as the last and the darkest bass-centered of his discography.

Hiraeth - 2020

A peak in JuffBass's creations. This album introduces drums for the very first time and either way keeps up the calm gentle theme of his earlier works.

Evenings On a Balcony - 2018

The second album was born due to unfulfilled idea the previous one couldn't bring to life. Its title is inspired by the settings its tracks were reviewed in, and listeners agree that it lives up to its name.

Moments With Birds - 2017

JuffBass's first album was the child of multiple improvisation jams that went so well he couldn't help but release them in a full-length format.
